Hamlin Hamlin & McGill's New Law Library


In 2004, a year after Charles McGill's suicide, Hamlin Hamlin McGill established a new law library. The Law Library will be named after Charles McGill. At the library's dedication, Howard Hamlin says in an interview with Joey Dixon's film crew that "it's been a tough year, but claims that HHM is back and will be Chuck's lasting legacy." To this day, the library still exists in the headquarters of Hamlin Hamlin McGill.
