Jack Barker to Replace Gavin Belson as CEO of Hooli Effective Immediately

There are unconfirmed reports that Belson violated Hooli protocols in the acquisition of a company that contained toxic assets. To that end, executives at Hooli voted to remove Belson effective immediately.

Belson will be succeeded by 'Action' Jack Barker, A veteran tech CEO, Barker commented "we at Hooli pledge to work with the Federal Trade Commision to resolve this matter and commit ourselves to earning back the public trust."
